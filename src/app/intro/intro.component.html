In the year 2670 the Golden Age of Mankind has come to an abrupt end. Their Interstellar Empire has been invaded by the Terrible Scourge. Almost all of humanity was wiped out. What was left escaped to the Cradle Worlds and grouped together to create the UCM (United Colonies of Mankind). They built and planned for 150 years before starting their reconquest. It is time for the reconquest to start it's biggest challenge, the end goal, now begins the Battle For Earth.

Dropzone Commander is a 10mm scale wargame, in which battles can be waged from small skirmishes, to epic battles which see huge waves of Dropships descend upon the battlefield. Players take command of one of five factions in an effort to ensure the fate of the Galaxy. Battles are fast paced and dynamic, with Dropships deploying and re-deploying units throughout the course of the engagement. This leads to tough tactical choices and exciting combat action.

Battles take place bewteen the United Colonies of Mankind (UCM) fighting to retake their home and the so called cradle worlds from the foul, neuro-parasistic aliens known as the Scourge. On these planets the Resistance has risen, the remnants of humanity left on these planets under Scourge occupation. The PHR, cybernetically enahnced humans, who escaped the Scourge invasion after heeding a warning from the mysterious White Sphere now temporarily helping the UCM and the Resistance in a fragile alliance. And finally, the Shaltari, ancient aliens who have technology so far advanced of all the others that they have cheated Death itself. Their desires still unknown.
