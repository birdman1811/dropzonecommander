Shaltari Tribes
The Shaltari are a race of aliens that were originally frinedly to humanity and in fact led them to the Cradle Worlds in the early days of humanitys exploration of the stars. However it soon came to pass the Shaltari were simply using humanity to fight wars amongst their own tribes. The Shaltari are an ancient race with exceptioanlly powerful but aliens technology. The Scourge opened communications for the first time with humanity recently simply to state that it was the Shaltari who lead the Scourge to earth. Once The Battle For Earth is won, no doubt the UCM will turn their attention to the Shaltari.

The Shaltari are an ancient, highly developed species. Their technology is advanced to a level of sophistication rivalled only by its potency. They have traversed the stars for millennia, learned myriad truths, and fought countless wars. Through knowledge, the Shaltari transcend even death itself, perceiving the ebb and flow of time like no other race.

In battle, the Shaltari are fearsome adversaries. Their swiftness is unmatched, their firepower unequalled, their bravery undeniable. The beauty of their constructs belies their true nature; instruments of death, honed to lethal perfection long before humanity learned to throw stones. They are peerless masters of technology.
